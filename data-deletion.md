# Data Deletion Instructions

**Last Updated: December 17, 2024**

## How to Delete Your Data from Splito

We respect your right to control your personal data. This page explains how you can delete your data from the Splito application.

---

## Option 1: Delete Account from the App (Recommended)

The easiest way to delete your account and all associated data is through the App:

1. Open the **Splito** app
2. Go to **Settings** (tap the gear icon)
3. Scroll down to the **Account** section
4. Tap **Delete Account**
5. Confirm your decision when prompted

### What Gets Deleted:

| Data Type | Deletion Status |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Your profile information (name, email, photo) | ✅ Permanently deleted |
| Your personal expenses | ✅ Permanently deleted |
| Your reminders | ✅ Permanently deleted |
| Your push notification tokens | ✅ Permanently deleted |
| Receipt images you uploaded | ✅ Permanently deleted |
| Your device-local cached data | ✅ Permanently deleted |

### What Happens to Group Data:

| Data Type | Status |
|-----------|--------|
| Groups you created (if you're the only member) | ✅ Permanently deleted |
| Groups with other members | ⚠️ Your membership is removed, but the group continues for other members |
| Expenses you added to shared groups | ⚠️ Expenses remain for other members' records (your name may be anonymized) |
| Your balance/settlement history in groups | ⚠️ Records remain for other members' financial accuracy |

---

## Option 2: Request Data Deletion via Email

If you cannot access the App, you can request data deletion by email:

1. Send an email to: **splito.team@gmail.com@gmail.com**
2. Use the subject line: **"Data Deletion Request - Splito"**
3. Include the following information:
   - Email address associated with your Splito account
   - Reason for deletion (optional)

### Processing Time:

- We will acknowledge your request within **48 hours**
- Your data will be permanently deleted within **30 days**
- You will receive confirmation once deletion is complete

---

## Data Retention After Deletion

### Immediately Deleted:

- Profile information
- Personal expenses
- Receipt images
- Push notification tokens
- Device-specific cached data

### Retained for Up to 30 Days:

- Backup copies (for disaster recovery purposes)
- Server logs containing your anonymized data

### May Be Retained:

- Aggregated, anonymized analytics data (cannot identify you)
- Data required for legal obligations

---

## Account Recovery

⚠️ **Warning:** Account deletion is **permanent and irreversible**.

Once your account is deleted:

- You cannot recover your data
- You cannot restore your expenses or groups
- You will need to create a new account if you want to use Splito again

---

## Third-Party Data

When you delete your account, we also remove your data from our third-party service providers:

| Service | Data Removed |
|---------|--------------|
| Firebase Authentication | Authentication credentials |
| Firebase Firestore | All user documents and subcollections |
| Firebase Storage | All uploaded receipt images |
| Firebase Analytics | Historical data is anonymized |
